Welcome to AI Model Benchmarking Framework

About Our Project

The AI Model Benchmarking Framework is a comprehensive tool designed to evaluate and compare the performance of various AI predictive models. Our platform allows researchers, data scientists, and businesses to upload their datasets and benchmark multiple AI models efficiently.

Key Features

  • User-friendly interface for dataset upload and model selection
  • Automated model training and evaluation process
  • Support for multiple AI models including KNN, LGBM, Logistic Regression, and Naive Bayes
  • Comprehensive performance metrics (accuracy, precision, recall, F1-score, ROC curves)
  • Advanced feature selection using Recursive Feature Elimination (RFE)
  • Visualizations for easy interpretation of results

How to Use Our Framework

  1. Upload Dataset: Click on "Upload Dataset" and select your file. Supported formats include CSV and JSON.
  2. Select Models: Choose the AI models you want to benchmark from our list of available options.
  3. Configure Settings: Set your preferences for feature selection, performance metrics, and other parameters.
  4. Run Benchmark: Click "Start Benchmark" to begin the automated process.
  5. View Results: Once complete, access detailed performance comparisons and visualizations on your dashboard.
  6. Export Report: Download a comprehensive report of your benchmarking results for further analysis.
